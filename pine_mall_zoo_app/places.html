Amazing Places to Explore

Discover incredible destinations and experiences throughout Pine City Zoo, from thrilling attractions to peaceful retreats and delicious dining options.

Amphitheatre

Entertainment

Catch an educational and entertaining event in our state-of-the-art outdoor amphitheatre. From wildlife presentations to musical performances, there's always something exciting happening for the whole family.

Live shows, educational presentations, and special events

Insect House

Discovery

Creepy crawlies of every kind you can imagine! Explore the fascinating world of insects in our climate-controlled habitat featuring butterflies, beetles, and exotic species from around the globe.

Interactive exhibits with butterflies and exotic insects

Monkey Trail

Adventure

Take a walk or ride through this challenging obstacle course designed to mimic monkey habitats. Perfect for active visitors looking for an adventure and a fun way to experience nature up close.

Adventure obstacle course for all ages

Wild Things Coffee Shop

Dining

Enjoy a cuppa or a dessert here. Our cozy coffee shop offers premium beverages, fresh pastries, and a perfect spot to rest and recharge during your zoo adventure.

Premium coffee, fresh pastries, and cozy atmosphere

Lost Forest

Nature

Relax and have a picnic under the trees by the lake. This peaceful sanctuary offers a perfect escape from the excitement with serene walking paths, picnic areas, and beautiful natural scenery.

Peaceful picnic areas and nature walking trails

Mo's Pizza

Dining

Scrumptuous pizzas and pastas for the family. Our wood-fired pizzas and authentic Italian dishes provide the perfect meal for hungry zoo explorers looking for hearty, delicious food.

Wood-fired pizza and authentic Italian cuisine

Dine @The Zoo

Fine Dining

Enjoy gourmet meals in a serene outdoor setting. Our flagship restaurant offers fine dining with panoramic views of the zoo's most beautiful landscapes and expertly crafted cuisine.

Gourmet dining with scenic zoo views
